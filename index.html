Destroy all tiles to reach next level.

When the ball is on the floor, the game is paused. Aim with the laser and shoot.

The ball does damage equal to its power.

More ricochets = stronger ball.
Special tiles give power-ups.

If the tiles reach the floor, you lose.
You can try again on the same level.
Progress is automatically saved.
